Dr. Leo Gilling

Criminologist • Educator • Community Leader

Biography

Dr. Leo Gilling is a qualified school executive, criminologist, philanthropist, and community leader. He holds a PhD in Criminology and Criminal Justice. His research focuses on contemporary issues in criminology, criminal justice, and forensic psychology, with a specialization in domestic violence and its societal impacts. He is active in diaspora education and public service initiatives.

Community Engagement

Beyond academia, Dr. Gilling remains active in community outreach programs aimed at reducing domestic violence and promoting restorative justice. His involvement underscores a practical application of his research and teachings, bridging the gap between theoretical frameworks and real-world impact.

Consulting Agency – CICCJ

The Caribbean Institute of Criminology and Criminal Justice (CICCJ) provides expert knowledge and strategic solutions to address the unique crime, security, and justice challenges within the Caribbean region. Services include advancing criminological understanding, policy advising, and regional training programs.
