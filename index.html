Biography

Dr. Leo Gilling is a qualified school executive, criminologist, philanthropist, and community leader. He holds a PhD in Criminology and Criminal Justice. His research focuses on contemporary issues in criminology, criminal justice, and forensic psychology, with a specialization in domestic violence and its societal impacts. He is active in diaspora education and public service initiatives.
