Dr. Leo Gilling

Criminologist • Educator • Community Leader

Email Download CV About

Dr. Leo Gilling is a distinguished educator, criminologist, and community leader known for his unwavering commitment to justice, education, and public service. Holding a Ph.D. in Criminology and Criminal Justice, he integrates academic expertise with real‑world impact — particularly through his work in university‑level teaching, diaspora education, and advocacy for Caribbean communities. His research and outreach focus on domestic violence, restorative justice, and building inclusive systems that empower underserved populations.
